Clean Shopper

Design System v1.0 — March 30, 2026

Trustworthy and approachable — a wellness-credible space that helps ingredient-conscious consumers research products with confidence. Generous layouts, natural color, and warm typography that communicates transparency without feeling clinical.

Design Principles

Credible, not clinical

Safety data without the lab report feel. Warmth and accuracy in equal measure.

Generous by default

Breathing room signals quality. Tight layouts feel anxious; generous ones feel considered.

Trust through consistency

Color carries meaning. Use semantic colors consistently so users never re-learn what green means.

Legible at every size

From hero headlines to EWG scores to badge labels — every text element reads comfortably.
